About Policom.

Policom is an online platform which displays news in a new visual way. Using data visualisation, I aim to show people news in a way which will help them fully understand what is going on.

The motive behind this major project concept was mainly because I feel that a lot of young people in particular feel disengaged with the political landscape and to a lesser extent even current events.

I have an interest in politics so it is good that I can design around a subject that I feel passionate about as I feel it is important to take an interest in what is going on around you, I fear other people within a similar age group to me don’t have that same political awareness.

Put simply, Policom is an idea. The idea being, visualising today’s news and politics in art format to make it easier to digest. I’ve created some art pieces which display various statistics and information from around the world

"By visualizing information, we turn it into a landscape that you can explore with your eyes..." - D. McCandless
